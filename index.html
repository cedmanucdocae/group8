About LeBron

LeBron James is a professional basketball player widely considered one of the greatest in the sport's history. Known for his strength, vision, and basketball IQ, LeBron has played for the Cleveland Cavaliers, Miami Heat, and Los Angeles Lakers. His impact goes beyond the court, with philanthropy and activism as central parts of his legacy.
